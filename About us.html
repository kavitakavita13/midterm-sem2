About us

Huda Beauty is a globally renowned cosmetics brand that has made a significant impact in the beauty industry. Founded by Huda Kattan, a makeup artist and beauty influencer, the brand is celebrated for its high-quality makeup products, particularly its eyeshadow palettes, liquid lipsticks, and false eyelashes. With a massive following on social media platforms like Instagram and YouTube, Huda Beauty has become synonymous with innovation and trendsetting in the beauty world.! One of the brand's standout products is the Desert Dusk Eyeshadow Palette, which boasts 18 shades inspired by the colors of the desert. This palette, along with other Huda Beauty eyeshadow collections, offers a mix of matte, shimmer, and glitter finishes, catering to a wide range of makeup looks. Another popular product is the Liquid Matte Lipstick, known for its long-lasting formula and extensive shade range. This lipstick has become a staple in many makeup collections for its ability to provide vibrant color that stays put throughout the day. Huda Beauty is also recognized for its Faux Mink Lash Collection, which offers false eyelashes that mimic the look of natural mink lashes. These lashes are favored for their ability to add volume and drama to the eyes without the need for extensions. In addition to makeup, Huda Beauty has ventured into skincare with the launch of the Wishful skincare line. This line includes products like the Yo Glow Enzyme Scrub and the Chin Lift Sculpting Sheet Mask, designed to address various skincare concerns and enhance the natural beauty of the skin.
